Our Story

About Parvati

Family-founded. Community-focused. Built to last.

Our Foundation

A Legacy Built Brick by Brick

Parvati Construction was founded in 1998 by Ravi Sharma, a first-generation immigrant who arrived in Texas with a set of tools, an unwavering work ethic, and a singular dream: to build something that lasts.

What started as a one-man operation doing residential repairs grew into a full-service construction firm employing over 45 skilled tradespeople. Today, Parvati Construction is helmed by Ravi alongside his daughter, Priya Sharma, who brings modern project management and sustainable building expertise to the family business.

We remain fiercely independent — no corporate ownership, no shortcuts. Every project is treated like it's the only one we have.

"We don't just build structures — we build trust, one project at a time."
— Ravi Sharma, Founder
